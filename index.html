ADAM'S LOUNGE
Est. 2024 · Bharatpur, Chitwan
Who We Are
Adam's Lounge is Chitwan's premier lounge destination — a space where luxury meets warmth, and every visit becomes a memory. Nestled in the heart of Bharatpur-3, we bring together the finest cocktails, curated imported spirits, international cuisine, and a relaxed hookah bar atmosphere under one elegant roof.
Our Story
Born from a passion for hospitality and a vision to elevate Chitwan's nightlife scene, Adam's Lounge was created to give the city a sophisticated space to unwind, connect, and celebrate. From intimate evenings to lively gatherings, we have crafted an environment where every guest feels at home — whether you're here for a quiet espresso, a craft cocktail, or a full dining experience with friends.
What We Offer
Our menu spans over 30 handcrafted cocktails, flaming showstoppers, a world-class selection of imported and domestic spirits, freshly brewed coffees, Nepali and international cuisines, wood-fired pizza, sizzlers, hookah sessions with premium flavours, and much more — making every evening a complete experience.
